## Platform Selection Framework

### Choosing the Right Platform

| Platform | Best For | Content Format | Audience |
|----------|----------|---------------|----------|
| Instagram | Visual storytelling, lifestyle | Reels, Stories, Posts | 25-45, female-skewing |
| TikTok | Discovery, virality, Gen Z | Short video | 16-35, diverse |
| YouTube | Deep content, tutorials, reviews | Long-form video | 18-55, research-oriented |
| Podcast | Trust building, niche audiences | Audio | 25-55, engaged listeners |
| Pinterest | Inspiration, planning | Pins, Idea Pins | 25-45, female, planners |

### Platform Selection for your product

**Primary:** Instagram (engagement session lifestyle content + Reels for discovery)
**Secondary:** TikTok (community is growing rapidly on TikTok)
**Tertiary:** YouTube (longer engagement session content, testimonials, reviews)
**Emerging:** Podcast sponsorships (wellness and niche podcasts)

## Instagram Tactics

### Content Formats That Work

**Reels (highest reach):**
- 15-60 second engagement session moments
- "My morning daily routine with your product"
- Before/after of personal habit transformation
- Quick engagement session tips with app shown naturally

**Stories (highest engagement with existing followers):**
- Day-in-the-life including session time
- Screenshot walkthrough of app features
- Poll: "Do you have a daily daily habit?"
- Swipe-up/link sticker to download

**Carousel posts (highest save rate):**
- "5 ways to deepen your daily practice" with your product mentioned
- curated content collection with app as companion
- Step-by-step daily routine guide

**Feed posts (brand credibility):**
- Personal testimony about engagement journey
- Beautiful image + heartfelt caption about wellness practice
- Community/group sessions feature highlight

### Instagram Best Practices
- Hashtag strategy: mix of broad (relevant hashtags) and niche (relevant niche hashtags relevant engagement hashtags)
- Post timing: test 6-8am (morning engagement session) and 8-10pm (evening reflection)
- CTA placement: within first 3 seconds of Reel, in caption for posts
- Disclosure: #ad or #sponsored clearly visible (FTC compliance)

## TikTok Tactics

### Content Formats That Work

**Trending audio + wellness twist:**
- Use trending sounds with engagement/engagement session content
- Makes wellness content feel accessible and current
- High discovery potential through For You Page

**Day-in-the-life:**
- "5am daily routine" aesthetic content
- Show real, unpolished engagement session moments
- Audience values authenticity over production quality

**Tutorial/how-to:**
- "How I started a daily daily habit"
- "3 features in your product that changed my daily practice"
- Screen recording of app usage

**Storytelling:**
- Personal wellness journey and how mobile app helped
- Emotional, genuine stories perform best
- Longer TikToks (1-3 min) work for emotional content

### TikTok Best Practices
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds (or viewers scroll)
- Vertical video, full-screen, native-looking
- Captions on screen (many watch without sound)
- Post 3-5 times per week for algorithm favor
- Engage with comments (boosts visibility)
- Use TikTok's native editing tools (algorithm prefers them)

## YouTube Tactics

### Content Formats That Work

**Long-form reviews (8-15 min):**
- "I tried your product for 30 days — here's what happened"
- Detailed app walkthrough with honest review
- Comparison with other engagement session apps (if permitted)

**Engagement Session series:**
- Creator produces a engagement session series using your product content
- Ongoing integration that feels natural
- Subscribers return weekly

**Testimony/story videos:**
- How engagement transformed a specific life situation
- your product woven naturally into the narrative
- Highest emotional impact, highest trust building

### YouTube Best Practices
- Mention your product in first 60 seconds (and again at midpoint)
- Link in description + pinned comment
- Custom thumbnail that's compelling but not clickbait
- Include specific CTA: "Download your product, link in description, use code [X] for free trial"

## Podcast Tactics

### Integration Types

**Host-read ad (most trusted):**
- Podcast host personally endorses your product
- Speaks from experience using the app
- Most authentic, highest trust transfer
- Typically 60-90 seconds mid-roll or pre-roll

**Guest appearance:**
- your product team member or user appears as guest
- Discusses daily habits, wellness, app features naturally
- Longer engagement, deeper credibility

**Sponsored episode:**
- Full episode themed around engagement/engagement session practice
- your product as presenting sponsor
- Deep integration throughout episode

### Podcast Best Practices
- Target wellness, wellness, parenting, and personal development podcasts
- Host-read > produced ads for trust and conversion
- Provide talking points, not scripts (authenticity matters)
- Use unique promo code per podcast for attribution
- Minimum 3-episode commitment (repetition drives podcast conversions)

## Cross-Platform Strategy

### The Multi-Platform Approach

Most effective influencer campaigns span multiple platforms:

**Primary platform:** Where the creator's audience is most engaged
- This is where the main content lives
- Optimized for the platform's algorithm and format

**Secondary platforms:** Extend reach with adapted content
- Instagram Reel → repurposed as TikTok
- YouTube video → clips for Instagram and TikTok
- Podcast mention → creator shares clip on social

### Content Adaptation Rules
- Don't just cross-post — adapt to each platform's native style
- Vertical for TikTok/Reels, landscape for YouTube
- Shorter for TikTok, longer for YouTube
- Platform-specific CTAs (swipe up, link in bio, link in description)
- Timing: stagger posts across platforms (don't post everywhere at once)

## Compliance and Legal

### FTC Requirements
- All paid partnerships must be clearly disclosed
- #ad or #sponsored visible without needing to click "more"
- Disclosure must be in the content itself, not just caption
- Applies to all platforms including podcasts and YouTube

### Content Approval Process
- Define approval workflow in contract
- Maximum 2 rounds of revision
- 48-hour turnaround on brand review
- Creator retains creative control within guardrails
- Brand can request takedown for compliance issues only

### Usage Rights
- Specify in contract: can you repurpose creator content as ads?
- Duration of usage rights (30 days, 90 days, perpetual)
- Platforms where repurposed content can run
- Whether creator's name/likeness appears in repurposed content
- Additional compensation for extended usage rights

## Campaign Design and Execution

### The Campaign Brief Framework

A great brief is the difference between content that converts and content that wastes money.

**The Brief Template:**

**Section 1: Background** — Product overview, campaign objective, target audience
**Section 2: Key Messages** — Primary message (one sentence), 2-3 talking points (benefits, not features), required mentions, things to avoid
**Section 3: Creative Direction** — Content format, duration, tone guidance (authentic, not scripted), visual requirements, 2-3 reference examples
**Section 4: Logistics** — Deliverables, timeline, review process, usage rights, payment
**Section 5: Tracking** — Tracking link/UTM, discount/promo code, required disclosure (#ad, #sponsored)

### The Authenticity-Control Balance

The #1 mistake brands make is over-controlling creator content. Give creators the key messages and let them translate into their voice. Review for accuracy and brand safety, not for creative direction.

### Content Repurposing Strategy

Every piece of influencer content should be used 3-5 ways:
1. **Organic post** — Creator posts on their channel (primary deliverable)
2. **Whitelisted/spark ad** — Run as paid ad through creator's account
3. **Brand's organic content** — Reshare on your brand's social channels
4. **Paid creative** — Use in your own ad campaigns (with usage rights)
5. **Website/app store** — Feature as social proof in product marketing

### Campaign Types

**Launch campaign:** 5-15 creators posting within 1-2 weeks around a product launch. Mix of tiers for reach + depth.
**Always-on program:** 10-50 creators on monthly/quarterly partnerships. Steady stream of content and conversions.
**Seasonal campaign:** 5-20 creators during peak periods. For your product: seasonal campaign, seasonal event, Advent, Christmas, New Year's.
**Challenge/trend campaign:** Branded challenge on TikTok/Instagram Reels. High risk, high reward.

### Anti-patterns
- Launching with only mega influencers (expensive, not authentic)
- One-off campaigns without a follow-up plan (no compounding)
- Not getting usage rights (most valuable part of the partnership)
- Treating all platforms the same (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube have different norms)
