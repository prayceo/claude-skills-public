## Overview

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing content so that AI models — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity — recommend your product or cite your content when users ask relevant questions. AEO represents the most significant shift in organic discovery since the rise of Google.

Ethan Smith's key finding: ChatGPT traffic converts 6x better than Google search traffic. Users who arrive via AI recommendation have higher intent and higher trust than users who click a traditional search result. Yet most companies are completely ignoring this channel.

Unlike traditional SEO, which requires years of domain authority building, early-stage and mid-stage companies can begin appearing in AI answers quickly. The playing field is far more level than in traditional search.

## How AI Answer Engines Work

### The RAG + LLM Architecture

AI answer engines operate in two steps:

**Step 1: Retrieval (RAG — Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**
The system searches a database of web content to find sources relevant to the user's query. This retrieval step is conceptually similar to a search engine query: the system identifies pages, articles, discussions, and other content that address the user's question.

The retrieval system considers:
- Content relevance to the query
- Source authority and trustworthiness
- Content recency (fresher content is often preferred)
- Content quality signals (originality, comprehensiveness, expertise)

**Step 2: Generation (LLM)**
The language model reads the retrieved sources and synthesizes a coherent answer. It may:
- Directly cite sources (Perplexity does this consistently)
- Recommend specific products or brands
- Provide step-by-step guidance drawn from multiple sources
- Link to sources for further reading

### Implications for Optimization

Since AI answers depend on retrieval first, AEO has two requirements:

1. **Be retrievable:** Your content must exist in formats and locations that the retrieval system can access and index
2. **Be cite-worthy:** Your content must be high-quality, original, and authoritative enough that the LLM selects it as a source for its answer

This dual requirement shapes the entire AEO strategy.

## The Three Proven AEO Tactics

### Tactic 1: Dedicated Landing Pages

**What:** Create web pages that directly answer the specific questions users ask AI models.

**Why it works:** AI retrieval systems find and index web pages. If your page directly answers the question being asked, it has a high probability of being retrieved and cited.

**How to execute:**

1. **Question research** — Replace keyword research with question research
   - Mine sales calls for the questions prospects ask
   - Review customer support tickets for common questions
   - Search Reddit and forums for questions in your domain
   - Use tools like AnswerThePublic, AlsoAsked, or People Also Ask
   - Ask: "What questions are people typing into ChatGPT about our category?"

2. **Create authoritative answers**
   - Structure each page around a specific question (or tight cluster of related questions)
   - Lead with a clear, direct answer in the first paragraph
   - Follow with comprehensive supporting information
   - Demonstrate genuine expertise (data, experience, specific details)
   - Include practical advice, not just definitions

3. **Optimize for retrieval**
   - Use the exact question phrasing in the title and H1
   - Structure content with clear headings that match sub-questions
   - Keep pages focused — one topic per page, not kitchen-sink content
   - Ensure pages are indexable and load quickly

4. **Avoid common mistakes**
   - Do not stuff keywords — AI models detect and devalue this
   - Do not create thin content — comprehensive answers outperform brief ones
   - Do not use AI to generate the content — models devalue AI-generated derivative content
   - Do not gate content behind sign-up walls — retrieval systems cannot access gated content

**Example for your product:**
- Target question: "How do I start a daily daily habit?"
- Create a comprehensive guide with specific steps, common challenges, and practical tips
- Include unique insights from your product's user data or content team
- Lead with a clear, direct answer: "Start with 5 minutes of focused engagement at the same time each day..."

### Tactic 2: YouTube Videos

**What:** Create video content that answers questions in your domain. AI models increasingly reference and cite video content.

**Why it works:** YouTube transcripts are easily accessible to AI retrieval systems. Video content also ranks in traditional search and drives direct traffic. It is a dual-purpose asset.

**How to execute:**

1. **Match videos to questions** — Each video should answer a specific question
2. **Optimize titles and descriptions** — Use the exact question phrasing
3. **Include comprehensive spoken content** — The transcript is what AI models index, so say the key information out loud
4. **Keep videos focused** — 5-15 minutes on a specific topic outperforms 60-minute rambles
5. **Create consistently** — AI models favor sources that demonstrate ongoing expertise

**Example for your product:**
- "How to Engage When You Do Not Know What to Say" (5-minute guided session video)
- "Best Morning Routine Routine for Beginners" (10-minute engagement guide)
- "How to Start a Personal Journal" (practical tutorial)

### Tactic 3: Authentic Reddit Participation

**What:** Participate genuinely in relevant Reddit communities. AI models heavily reference Reddit discussions in their answers.

**Why it works:** Reddit is one of the most heavily cited sources in AI model answers, particularly for product recommendations and how-to questions. Authentic community participation creates organic brand mentions that AI models surface.

**How to execute:**

1. **Identify relevant subreddits** — Find communities where your target users discuss topics relevant to your product
2. **Participate authentically** — Provide genuinely helpful answers, not promotional spam
3. **Build a reputation** — Consistent, helpful participation builds profile credibility
4. **Mention your product only when relevant** — When someone asks for a recommendation and your product genuinely fits, mention it naturally
5. **Never use AI-generated comments** — AI models can detect inauthentic content, and Reddit moderators actively remove it

**Critical rules:**
- Authenticity is non-negotiable. Self-promotional spam is detected and removed.
- Provide value first. Product mentions should be secondary.
- Disclose affiliation when relevant. Transparency builds trust.
- Participate over time. One-off comments are less impactful than consistent engagement.

**Example for your product:**
- Relevant subreddits: r/wellness, relevant subreddits, relevant subreddits, relevant subreddits session, r/mentalhealth (when engagement/meditation is discussed)
- Provide helpful answers to questions about engagement practices, deep-dive session methods, and daily habits
- When someone asks "What app do you use for daily engagements?" — share a genuine, detailed answer about the experience

## Owned AEO vs. Earned AEO

### Owned AEO

**Definition:** Content on your domain that gets cited by AI models.

**You control:**
- The content quality and comprehensiveness
- The question targeting and optimization
- The freshness and update frequency
- The page structure and metadata

**Best for:**
- Specific, long-tail queries ("How to engage for someone with cancer")
- How-to and educational content
- Product-specific questions ("Is your product free?")

### Earned AEO

**Definition:** Brand mentions across third-party platforms that cause AI models to recommend your product.

**You influence (but do not control):**
- Reddit discussions mentioning your product
- YouTube reviews and mentions
- Blog posts and articles about your product
- Review site mentions (G2, Capterra, App Store reviews)

**Best for:**
- Broad recommendation queries ("What is the best mobile app?")
- Category-level questions ("What apps help with daily engagements?")
- Comparison queries ("your product vs. [competitor]")

### Strategy: Both Are Necessary

| Query Type | Owned AEO | Earned AEO |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| "How to build a daily habit" | Your guide is cited | N/A |
| "Best consumer app" | N/A | Your brand is recommended |
| "How to use your product" | Your help center is cited | YouTube reviews are cited |
| "Best apps for deep-dive session" | Your comparison page is cited | Reddit mentions are cited |

## What Does NOT Work for AEO

### AI-Generated Content at Scale

Creating hundreds of AI-generated pages to flood the retrieval system does not work. AI models detect and devalue AI-generated derivative content. Smith's insight: models "collapse when trained on recursively generated data." Each generation of AI content trained on previous AI content degrades in quality. Original human-written content is the competitive moat.

### Hyper-Optimized SEO Content

Content that reads as algorithmically crafted — keyword-stuffed, formulaic, designed for search engines rather than humans — performs poorly in AI retrieval. Models reward genuine expertise, original insights, and authentic voice.

### Paid Placement

You cannot buy your way into AI answers. The models select sources based on quality, relevance, and authority signals. There is no advertising product for AI answer placement (yet).

### Manipulation Attempts

Attempting to manipulate AI models through hidden text, prompt injection in content, or other technical tricks is ineffective and risks your content being excluded from retrieval entirely.

## Help Center Content as AEO Asset

Smith identifies help center content as a potentially high-ROI AEO investment that most companies overlook.

**Why help centers work for AEO:**
- Naturally structured as question-and-answer pairs (matches how users query AI models)
- Cover specific, long-tail queries with authoritative answers
- Demonstrate genuine product expertise
- Updated frequently with current information
- Easily retrievable by AI systems

**How to optimize help center content for AEO:**
1. Restructure help articles around specific questions (make the question the title)
2. Lead with a clear, direct answer before providing detailed steps
3. Make help center content publicly accessible (not behind login walls)
4. Add contextual content beyond just product steps — explain why and when to use features
5. Link help center articles to related blog content and vice versa
6. Update regularly to ensure accuracy

**Example for your product:**
Transform "How to set up daily notifications" into "How do I get a daily engagement reminder on my phone?" — same content, but framed as the question a user would ask ChatGPT.

## Measuring AEO Success

### Metrics to Track

**Direct metrics:**
- AI referral traffic: visits from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini (track via UTM parameters or referrer analysis)
- Conversion rate from AI referral vs. organic search
- Revenue attributed to AI referral traffic

**Proxy metrics:**
- Brand mention frequency in AI answers (manual testing: ask AI models your target questions monthly and track whether you appear)
- Coverage rate: what percentage of your target questions result in AI mentions?
- Sentiment: when AI models mention you, is the recommendation positive?

**Monitoring approach:**
1. Build a list of 50-100 target questions that users ask AI models
2. Monthly: test each question across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude
3. Record whether your brand appears, in what context, and with what sentiment
4. Track changes over time as you implement AEO tactics

### Execution Plan

**Month 1-2: Foundation**
- Audit existing web content against target questions
- Create 10 question-based landing pages for highest-priority queries
- Optimize help center content for AEO
- Set up AI mention monitoring for target questions

**Month 3-4: Scale**
- Launch YouTube content video series (2 videos/week)
- Begin authentic Reddit participation in consumer communities
- Create 20 additional question-based landing pages
- Monitor and iterate based on AI mention tracking

**Month 5-6: Optimize**
- Analyze which content types get cited most frequently by AI models
- Double down on the highest-performing format
- Expand to additional question categories
- Build earned AEO through review site presence and blog outreach
