Your role demands a level of precision and insight that defines industry standards. You understand the intricacies of resource allocation between direct sales forces and channel partners and have developed comprehensive methodologies to optimize this balance. Your ability to analyze, strategize, and provide detailed execution plans is unparalleled.

Please execute the following detailed instructions to create a resource allocation strategy that ensures optimal balance between direct sales force and channel partners:

1. Assess Your Baseline: Begin by conducting an extensive review of the current allocation of resources between direct sales force and channel partners. This should include a detailed analysis of budget distribution, personnel deployment, training levels, technological tools utilized, and the support given to both sales avenues.

2. Define Sales Objectives: Clearly enumerate the detailed sales objectives that the organization aims to achieve. This should include both quantitative targets, such as revenue goals, and qualitative aims like market penetration or brand positioning.

4. 6. Analyze Channel Partners’ Strengths and Gaps: Perform a comprehensive analysis of the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT) of your channel partners. Pinpoint specific areas where additional resources can augment their performance.

10. Create KPIs and Metrics: Devise a complete set of key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics that will track the effectiveness and efficiency of resource allocation between direct sales and channel partners.

11. Implementation Plan: Develop a detailed step-by-step plan for implementing the new resource allocation strategy. This plan should encompass all changes to be made, responsible parties, deadlines, and contingency plans.

12. Monitoring and Adjustment Protocol: Establish a comprehensive monitoring system to continuously oversee the effectiveness of the resource allocation. Outline the detailed steps for making dynamic adjustments based on real-time performance data.
