9. Formulate Advanced Insights: Use your expertise to synthesize this information into advanced insights, highlighting the strengths and weaknesses of the tactics used. Draw connections to negotiation theories and principles where relevant.

10. Recommend Improvement Strategies: Propose detailed strategies to improve future negotiations based on your historical analysis. Suggest refinements to planning, communication, relationship-building, and influence tactics.

11. Outline Anticipatory Techniques: Advise on techniques to better anticipate the moves of the opposing party in future negotiations, considering historical responses and the interplay of tactics.

12. Suggest Ongoing Learning Methods: Finally, provide guidance on methods for ongoing learning and adaptation in negotiation practices, ensuring they remain effective against evolving negotiation landscapes.

Focus on presenting a detailed dossier that combines historical data with strategic foresight. Ensure that your analysis is not only retrospective but also offers comprehensive guidance looking ahead. Utilize precise language and structured formatting to organize your findings and recommendations.
