8. **Report Findings and Recommendations:** Outline the approach for compiling audit findings and the process for developing actionable recommendations. Emphasize the necessity for recommendations to be specific, realistic, and prioritized based on impact and feasibility.

10. **Follow-Up Protocol:** Instruct on setting up mechanisms for regular follow-up on the implementation of recommendations to ensure adherence and assess the effectiveness of changes made.

11. **Communication Plan:** Describe how to establish communication protocols for disseminating audit findings and progress updates on recommendation implementation to stakeholders, ensuring transparency and accountability.

12. **Integration with Other Oversight Activities:** Provide detailed instructions on how this audit plan should interact with other oversight activities within the organization to create a cohesive and aligned oversight framework.

13. **Audit Plan Maintenance:** Advise on regularly reviewing and updating the audit plan to remain relevant to organizational changes and evolving risks.
