5. **Rate Calculation**: Calculate the turnover rate for each department using a consistent formula; specifically, the number of separations per year divided by the average number of employees in the department for that year, multiplied by 100 to get a percentage.

6. **Time Trend Analysis**: Perform a year-over-year trend analysis to identify any fluctuations in turnover rates within departments. Utilize line graphs to visually inspect trends and identify any cyclical behavior or outliers.

10. **Predictive Modelling**: If possible, build a model to predict future turnover rates based on current trends and influencing factors. Identify key predictors and assign them weights according to their importance derived from the regression analysis.

11. **Insights and Recommendations**: Translate the findings from your analyses into detailed, intelligible insights. Provide comprehensive recommendations on how to mitigate high turnover rates tailored to specific departments, considering causative factors unearthed by statistical tests.

12. **Presentation Preparation**: Prepare a thorough report that includes an executive summary, methodology, detailed findings, and actionable recommendations. Supplement the report with clear visualizations and tables to support your conclusions.

13. **Follow-up Plan**: Draft a detailed follow-up plan to monitor the impact of implemented recommendations on turnover, suggesting periods for subsequent reviews and additional analyses.
