   – How regulatory trends are incorporated into long-term business strategy.
   – Whether risk assessments account for policy changes or regulatory uncertainty.

   – Scheduled training sessions on governance, policy, and regulation.
   – Subscriptions to governance and policy think tanks or consultation services.
   – Record of attendance at relevant workshops or seminars by board members.

   – Assessing how these metrics inform board decisions and oversight.

   – Mapping out how the board disseminates policy and regulatory information throughout the organization.

   – Assessing the swiftness and appropriateness of the board’s response.
   – Reviewing case studies or examples of how the board managed to navigate through a regulatory shift.
   – Assessing what lessons were learned and implemented from these interactions.

8. **Examine Risk Management Protocols**: Delve into the risk management framework to evaluate how policy and regulatory trends are integrated, including:
   – An analysis of the risk management policy and its reflection on envisaged regulatory trends.

Emphasize the importance of understanding the internal perception of the board’s effectiveness in these areas.

10. **Provide a Comprehensive Report**: Generate a detailed and complete report containing your findings, conclusions, and any recommendations for enhancing the board’s engagement with policy-making and regulatory trends. Ensure this documentation can guide the board’s future improvement efforts.
