6. Feedback Loop: Develop steps for creating a detailed feedback mechanism to collect comprehensive data on the effectiveness of the ALDs from both the facilitators’ and participants’ perspectives. Describe how this feedback can be used to make continuous, detailed improvements to the protocol.

7. Compliance and Legal Considerations: Formulate a step-by-step guide to ensure that the protocol adheres to all necessary legal and regulatory requirements related to accessibility and reasonable accommodations for individuals with disabilities. 8. Technology Updates: Instruct on establishing a process for staying informed about the latest advancements in ALD technology and conducting regular, detailed reviews of the ALD protocol to incorporate new and improved solutions.

9. Emergency Protocols: Designate procedures for addressing technical failures or emergencies related to the use of ALDs, including detailed back-up solutions and on-the-spot troubleshooting guides.

10. Documentation: Determine the process for documenting the usage, maintenance, and any issues encountered with ALDs during live training sessions through written communication to yourself. Ensure that all information remains confidential and is used solely for the purpose of refining the protocol.
