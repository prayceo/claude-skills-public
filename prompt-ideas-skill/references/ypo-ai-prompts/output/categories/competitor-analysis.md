4. Elaborate on the approach to assess the impact of Competitor E’s sales promotions, detailing which metrics should be examined. Consideration should be given to immediate and long-term effects, examining sales volume, market share, customer acquisition, customer retention, and any alterations in consumer buying behavior directly attributable to the promotions.

5. Provide guidance on benchmarking the findings from Competitor E against industry standards and our own promotional efforts. 7. Define how you would maintain a continually updated understanding of Competitor E’s promotion activities, suggesting how to adapt your methodology in response to new data or market developments without creating new systems or documentation processes.

8. Lastly, propose a system for providing periodic updates and insights to key stakeholders within our organization through written communication, which will channel the evaluation, conclusions, and recommendations based on the ongoing competitor analysis.

Your systematic approach and detailed instructions are critical for extracting valuable intelligence from Competitor E’s sales promotion strategies. Please remember, the execution of these strategies, ensuring confidentiality and strategic advantage, rests on your capacity to communicate these insights through direct channels to our organization’s decision-makers.
