8. Design a monitoring system to periodically assess the engagement levels, in tandem with the risks previously identified. This system should allow for timely detection of engagement slippage and quick implementation of pre-emptive measures.

9. Prepare detailed guidance on initiating a culture shift towards higher engagement through leadership training, communication enhancement, and the reinforcement of positive behaviors.

10. Conclude your report with an executive summary that encapsulates the most significant findings and provides clear, decisive recommendations for stakeholder action.
