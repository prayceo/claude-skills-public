10. Evaluate and Adapt Webinar Participation Strategy: Regularly analyze the effectiveness of the webinar participation strategy, making comprehensive adjustments for continuous improvement.

11. Identify metrics for comprehensive system evaluation. This includes tracking participation rates, satisfaction scores, and impacts on team cohesion and sales results. These should form a benchmarking report template that the user can apply regularly.

12. Construct an adaptable update protocol for the peer-recognition criteria, ensuring the system remains relevant, aligned with evolving sales goals, and capable of incorporating feedback from the team.

Be exhaustive in your elaboration on each step, and make sure each is couched in terms that reflect a thorough grasp of team building and the sales environment. The focus of this task is to yield a peer-recognition system that is both robust and bespoke to the unique ecosystem of a sales team, fostering a culture where recognition is both meaningful and effective.
